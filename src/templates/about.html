About

GLE is an initiative of the Rothschild Foundation Hanadiv Europe and the National Library of Israel

It’s aim is to create opportunities for European scholars, library and museum professionals, educators and community activists working within Jewish settings to develop an on-going, mutually beneficial relationship with the NLI and to engage with the NLI's diverse and extensive collections.

Recent initiatives include:

At the Source

Jewish European Ephemera Collection: A window into Jewish life, bringing contemporary ephemera – printed materials with a short life-span, such as invitations, flyers, greeting cards, notices and tickets – from Jewish communities around Europe.
